Decision Making

Turn overwhelm into one clear next step

Handled is a decision-making app built for busy, distracted minds. It breaks choices into bite-sized actions so you can move forward without spiraling.

Split the decision

Handled separates the decision from the noise, clarifies the goal, and identifies what matters most.

Score your options

We map pros, cons, and effort, then surface the most realistic next step based on your energy.

Get a single action

Instead of dumping advice, Handled gives one small action you can finish in minutes.

Example: "Should I take the meeting?" becomes a 3-step plan and a confidence meter so you can decide fast.
